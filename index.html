Vision & Mission

SARC provides platform for students to interact with well settled alumni's of NITJ. It aids in gaining career advice and gives them direction towards better future.SARC also organise alumni meet every year. It is a one day get together of all the passed out batches that involves various fun and productive events alongside a round table interaction with the esteemed Director and other respected dignitaries. Rendering students with career guidance and tailoring them for their future endeavours. Catering to the betterment of alumni-student relationship of Institute. To furnish alumni with a platform to interact with the students on a one to one basis and connect them with the Institute and provide career advice to prepare for the real world.

Director's Message

Dear Alumnus,

It is an honor to be associated with NIT Jalandhar community. I am grateful to have this opportunity to lead team of young learners, faculty and staff members in their endeavour to raise NIT Jalandhar to its rightful place as one of the premier Institute of country. The success of any Institute is associated with strength of creative capacity of its students,faculty and alumni. The faculty and staff members are doing their part in building rich intellectual potential contributing to the society. It gives me immense pleasure to find NITJ Alumni in diverse field comprising administration, IT, marketing and entrepreneurship in big numbers. They have been making their mark everywhere. The Institute would like to utilize the potential of our alumni in achieving its mission to offer state of the art technical knowledge for sustained growth of the country.
